S-1 is the Self-Organizing Human-Machine Network

ABSTRACT

The convergence of autonomous governance systems with decentralized artificial intelligence presents an opportunity for the fundamental reshaping of legacy socio-economic structures. S-1 postulates a model for a self-organizing human-machine network that integrates network states, decentralized AI (de-AI), and cryptographic primitives within a framework of parallel economies. The Network State, a digital-first community governed by decentralized systems that can eventually acquire and crowdfund physical territory, serves as the foundational layer upon which all governance and socio-economic functions rest.
